# SubscriTrack: Subscription Revenue & Churn Analyzer

SubscriTrack is a data analytics project designed to help subscription-based businesses understand their **revenue trends, customer churn behavior, and key business metrics**. Using Python-based data analysis and visualization, the project identifies high-risk customer segments and provides actionable insights to improve retention and recurring revenue.

The analysis is performed using the **Telco Customer Churn Dataset**, a widely used dataset for churn analysis and business analytics projects.

---

 Project Objectives

* Analyze subscription revenue performance
* Measure customer churn rate and revenue churn
* Identify high-risk customer segments
* Track Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
* Provide data-driven insights to reduce churn and increase retention

---

 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

This project calculates important subscription business metrics:

* Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) – Total monthly revenue from active customers
* Customer Churn Rate – Percentage of customers who cancel their subscriptions
* Revenue Churn Rate – Revenue lost due to churned customers
* Average Revenue per User (ARPU) – Average monthly revenue per active user
* Active vs Churned Customers – Distribution of retained and lost customers

---

 Key Insights

Some insights generated from the analysis include:

* Customers with month-to-month contracts have the highest churn rate
* *New customers (low tenure) are more likely to churn
* Certain payment methods are associated with higher churn
* Higher monthly charges can increase the probability of churn

These insights help businesses design **better retention strategies and pricing models**.
